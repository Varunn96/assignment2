Venue of the Formula One Belgian Grand Prix and Spa 24 Hours endurance race.

Designed in 1920 by Jules de Thier and Henri Langlois Van Ophem, the original triangle-shaped course used public roads between the Belgian towns of Francorchamps, Malmedy, and Stavelot.

The most famous part of the circuit is the Eau Rouge / Raidillon combination.
